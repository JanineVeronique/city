About Port- of- Spain

Port- of- Spain is the capital city of Trinidad and Tobago. It is the second largest city in the country. In addition Port- of- Spain is also the financial and administrative centre of the country. Interestingly, the main part of downtown Port-of-Spain was originally engulfed by tidal mudflats and covered by mangroves. On average the weather in this city reaches the highest 98.6 degrees F and lowest 58. 3 degrees F. The main buildings in the city are the Red House which is where Parliament is held, the National and Performing Arts Centre where main cultural events are held, and the Twin Towers where the Central Bank of Trinidad and Tobago resides.
